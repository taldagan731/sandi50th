# Still Becoming — Project Bible

## Purpose
A private interactive documentary and 50th-birthday film for Sandi Yadegari, born August 11, 1976.

## Submission deadline
August 7, 2026.

## Brand
Pink and purple are Sandi's favorite colors. The experience should feel literary, cinematic, elegant, and personal.

## Story chapters
1. Once Upon a Time
2. Growing Up in Roslyn
3. Finding Her Voice
4. Building Something Bigger
5. The Family She Chose
6. Around the World
7. The People Who Love Her
8. Still Becoming
